The History and Geography of Wristwatches

For GEOG 572, I developed a StoryMap that narrates a visual story of personal significance. This project prompted me to further investigate European history and to reflect on how human experience is shaped and remembered over time. In particular, I conducted in-depth research on the global watch market to construct a narrative tracing the growing cultural and economic significance of wristwatches.
