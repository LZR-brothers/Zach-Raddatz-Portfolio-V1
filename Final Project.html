Arc of Time Design Protype Maps

For my final project, I am designing an atlas that traces a factory to reef narrative linking Swiss luxury watch manufacturing sites to a selected portion of the Great Barrier Reef. The work shown here consists of figures that will be placed into future atlas spreads, including reef maps at regional and local scales, a map of Swiss production locations, and supporting reference graphics. The atlas follows one dive watch from the controlled geographies of manufacturing in Switzerland to the marine environment where it is used. Each destination spread will also include a space for a user photograph of their watch at that site and a blank text box for written reflection, so the final atlas functions as both a cartographic reference and a personal field record.
